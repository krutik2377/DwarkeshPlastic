## üé≠ Animation Techniques Used

### 1. **Framer Motion Animations**
- `initial`, `animate`, `transition` for entrance animations
- `whileHover` for hover states
- `whileTap` for click feedback
- `useInView` for scroll-triggered animations
- `useMotionValue` and `useTransform` for advanced effects

### 2. **CSS Animations**
- `@keyframes` for continuous animations
- `transition` for smooth state changes
- `transform` for 3D effects
- `backdrop-filter` for glassmorphism

### 3. **Advanced Techniques**
- **Staggered Animations**: Delays create sequential appearance
- **Spring Physics**: Natural bouncy animations
- **Infinite Loops**: Continuous subtle movements
- **Conditional Animations**: Animations trigger on specific states
- **Parallax Effects**: Different layers move at different speeds
